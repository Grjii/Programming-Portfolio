Sam's Alfredo Pasta Recipe

This Garlic Bacon Shrimp Alfredo is a rich and flavorful dish featuring creamy sauce, crispy bacon, and tender shrimp, making it a perfect indulgence for any occasion.

Necessary Ingredients:

  • 6 slices of bacon, chopped
  • 1 lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
  • 1/2 medium yellow onion, diced (124 grams)
  • 1 medium tomato, diced (61 grams)
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups heavy cream (240 grams)
  • 1 lb fettuccine, cooked
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan cheese (113 grams)
  • 1/2 cup fresh parsley, chopped (30 grams)

Method:

  1. In a large pot, cook the bacon over medium heat until crispy.
  2. Add the shrimp, salt, and pepper. Cook until the shrimp turns pink and opaque. Remove the shrimp and bacon from the pot, leaving the bacon fat behind.
  3. Add the onions, tomatoes, and garlic to the pot. Cook until the garlic is lightly browned.
  4. Pour in the heavy cream and bring to a boil.
  5. Once boiling, add the fettuccine. Return the shrimp and bacon to the pan, then stir in the Parmesan cheese and parsley until the cheese melts and the sauce evenly coats the pasta.
  6. Serve hot and enjoy.
