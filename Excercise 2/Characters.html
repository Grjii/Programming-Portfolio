How I Met Your Mother

Plot Cast Characters Reviews

Notable Characters

  • Ted Mosby: The protagonist, a hopeless romantic.
  • Marshall Eriksen: Ted’s best friend, a lawyer.
  • Lily Aldrin: Marshall’s wife, a kindergarten teacher.
  • Barney Stinson: A suit-wearing playboy with a love for challenges.
  • Robin Scherbatsky: A Canadian journalist and love interest of both Ted and Barney.
  • Tracy McConnell: Ted’s eventual wife and the mother of his children.
